Our story

Less, but better.

NOIR was founded on a single conviction: most things are unnecessary. What remains — the truly considered, the precisely made, the quietly essential — is worth celebrating.

This store is a portfolio project built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It demonstrates a complete e-commerce flow: product listing, filtering, sorting, a product detail page, an interactive cart, and a multi-step checkout with form validation.

Every interaction, animation, and layout decision was crafted intentionally — no frameworks, no dependencies, just clean code.
